# Card Icon Art Review Rules

All card icons should pass this visual gate before being used in game.

- Clear at 48px: the core action must be readable when scaled down.
- Solid functional silhouette: prefer bold white shapes with clean negative cuts over thin decorative line art.
- Mature game icon feel: similar quality bar to weapon mod, tactical trait, and RPG skill icons.
- Unified system: black base, warm white icon, black internal cut lines, subtle card-color accent only on frame/details.
- Semantic first: punch cards must look like a fist, gun cards must look like a gun, movement cards must look like the protagonist moving.
- No crude block shapes: avoid rectangles that only vaguely imply the action.
- No noisy shadows: use controlled black outline/drop shadow, not duplicated offset silhouettes.
- No text or tiny details inside the icon.
